ABOUT US

DMJ RECYCLING LTD is a family run company, where we understood how important are customer service, punctuality and diligence. We are collecting scrap metal and cables that would otherwise be thrown away as trash, which then are turning into new products. We offer free collection service from construction and demolition sites, houses etc. We cover Reading, London, Luton, Gatwick.
